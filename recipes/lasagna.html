Lasgna

Description

This is my mom’s beloved homemade lasagna recipe, featuring a rich, meaty tomato sauce made from scratch and a creamy, cheesy filling. While traditional lasagna often uses ricotta, her version uses a flavorful mix of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan — and I’ve yet to find a better one anywhere. It’s perfect served with a fresh green salad and some crusty garlic bread. Making homemade lasagna might look complicated, but it’s easier than you think. Don’t let the layers of bold Italian flavor intimidate you — give this recipe a try today!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna
