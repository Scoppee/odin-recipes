Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi fried rice (also known as kimchi-bokkeumbap) is a flavorful and adaptable Korean dish that's bound to become a go-to favorite. Easily customize it by swapping ground beef for ham, bacon, or spam, tossing in extra vegetables, or turning up the heat with a spoonful of Korean gochujang paste.

Ingredients

  • Oils
  • Beef
  • Green onion
  • Kimchi
  • Gochujang
  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and the white parts of the green onion in canola oil.
  2. Add the kimchi and gochujang, cook until warmed through, then stir in the rice.
  3. Cook until the rice is heated through, then finish with sesame oil and green onions.
  4. Cook the egg and serve it over the kimchi fried rice.
